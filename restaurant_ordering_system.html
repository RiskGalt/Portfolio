Designed for real restaurant operations

We don’t build generic menus. We map your actual service model — dine-in, takeout, delivery, bar, modifiers, combos — and turn it into a clean ordering flow that your team can use under pressure.

Data-ready by default

Every order is more than a ticket. We design the underlying data model so you can later answer questions like “What items stall the kitchen?”, “What modifiers drive margin?”, and “Where do we lose tickets?”

  • Consistent IDs for items, modifiers, locations, and channels
  • Event-level tracking of order creation, fire, and completion
  • Schemas that can be exported cleanly into BI tools

Built to integrate, not replace everything you have
