Online menu + WhatsApp ordering for busy neighbourhood restaurants.

Guests tap through a clean menu, build their order, and send it straight to your WhatsApp. No apps, no logins, fewer phone calls, fewer mistakes.

Talk about a menu for your restaurant View live demo

Built for takeout, cash discounts, and small teams.

This is not a marketplace app or a big POS replacement. It’s a focused online menu designed for restaurants where:

  • Most orders are takeout or pickup
  • You offer a cash discount or in-store deals
  • Your phone lines get jammed during the lunch or dinner rush
  • You and your team already live in WhatsApp

Customers browse by category (Appetizers, Mains, Noodles, Rice, Lunch Specials, etc.), tap the dishes they want, and send the finished order directly to your WhatsApp.

How the online menu + WhatsApp flow works

  1. Your guest scans a QR code or taps a link
    They land on a clean, mobile-first menu branded for your restaurant.
  2. They choose their dishes by category
    Dishes are grouped into logical sections (e.g., Appetizers, Chicken, Beef, Fish, Noodles, Rice, Lunch). No endless scroll – each section can expand / collapse.
  3. They add items to a simple cart
    Quantities update in real time; totals are visible so there are no surprises.
  4. They enter their name and send the order via WhatsApp
    The full ticket (items, quantities, total, customer name) arrives in your WhatsApp chat.
  5. Your team confirms and prepares the order
    You can reply with approximate time or pickup instructions right inside WhatsApp.

Why this works well for mom-and-pop restaurants

  • Fewer errors on the phone.
    Guests choose their own dishes and quantities. No misheard items over a noisy line.
  • No new apps for staff to learn.
    Orders arrive in WhatsApp, where you already coordinate with staff and suppliers.
  • Cash discount still front and centre.
    Messaging can highlight “Cash discount on pickup” instead of pushing delivery fees.
  • No per-order percentage fees.
    It’s your site, your menu, and your WhatsApp number – not a third-party marketplace.
  • Ready for future data & reporting.
    Even if we start simple, the menu is built on a structured item model, so later you can plug in analytics (best sellers, slow movers, peak periods, etc.).

Data-ready under the hood, even if you start small.

Behind the scenes, each dish has a consistent ID and category. As we layer in analytics (for example, in Google Analytics or a separate dashboard), we can answer questions like:

  • Which dishes are ordered most often, and at what times?
  • Which categories peak during lunch vs. dinner?
  • Which items hardly ever get ordered (slow movers)?
  • From which devices are people ordering – mobile vs. desktop?

For now, we focus on getting you a working, clean online menu. Later, you can choose to add deeper reporting and insights.

What’s included when we build your online menu

We typically price this as a straightforward project fee for a single location. Exact numbers can change, but a typical engagement includes:

  • Setup of a branded online menu page with your logo and colours
  • Menu structure (categories + up to ~150–180 dishes entered for you)
  • WhatsApp order integration with your restaurant’s number
  • Basic Google Analytics setup (page views, clicks, category interest)
  • Minor text and menu adjustments after launch

Hosting can be on our side or yours. If you already have a website, we can integrate the menu as a sub-page; if not, we can host it under our infrastructure and simply give you a link and QR code to print on menus and takeout boxes.
